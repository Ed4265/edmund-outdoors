Our Services

We offer a range of specialized outdoor activities tailored for enthusiasts of all skill levels. Explore our services below:

Hunting and Shooting:

  • Guided Deer Stalking (Roe Deer) - Ideal for both beginners and seasoned stalkers.
  • Guided Fox Shooting - Essential for population control and environmental management.
  • Rabbit Shooting - Enjoy this traditional activity perfect for groups.
  • Guided Pigeon Shooting - Challenge your marksmanship with flying targets.
  • Guided Crow Shooting - Test your skills against these intelligent birds.
  • Rat Hunting - Aimed at controlling vermin populations effectively.
  • Duck/Goose Flights - Engage in seasonal waterfowl hunting.

Falconry:

Bring your hawks for flights on hares, pheasant, and partridge - a thrilling experience for falconry enthusiasts.

Fishing:

  • Guided Pike Fishing - Target impressive double-figure pike in well-stocked waters.
  • Guided River Carp Fishing - Discover the techniques for capturing elusive carp.
  • Barbel Fishing Lessons - Learn the secrets to successful barbel fishing from an expert.

Other Activities:

  • Ferreting - Dive into the world of traditional rabbit hunting with ferrets.
  • Nature Walks - Explore the beauty of the local environment with guided nature walks.
  • Metal Detecting Sessions - Access hundreds of fields and discover hidden treasures with our metal detecting adventures.
