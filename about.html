About Me

Hello, I'm a lifelong outdoorsman with over thirty years of experience in hunting and wildlife conservation. From the age of ten, the great outdoors has been my classroom and sanctuary. Whether it was fishing in quiet streams, hunting rabbits, or exploring the rugged terrains of Dartmoor and the Cairngorms, every adventure fueled my passion for nature.

My journey has not only honed my skills but also deepened my respect for the balance of nature and the ethical role hunters play in conservation. Over the decades, I've mastered techniques alongside some of Europe's finest gamekeepers and hunters, and my experiences range from controlling fox populations with my trusted dogs to tracking the largest wild boar and deer across diverse landscapes.

In 2019, I took my passion online, documenting my adventures on social media and quickly connecting with thousands of fellow hunting enthusiasts and nature lovers. This online community continues to grow, sharing stories, skills, and the sheer excitement of the hunt.

What I Offer

I provide tailored hunting experiences that cater to both seasoned hunters and novices eager to learn. Through my books and guided outings, I share the skills and insights gained from a lifetime of pursuits. Join me for an unforgettable journey into the wild, where we'll explore the art and spirit of hunting and deepen our connection with the outdoors.

Whether you're looking to sharpen your hunting skills or simply want to experience the thrill of the hunt in a responsible and sustainable manner, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.
